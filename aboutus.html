About Us

Our story

Tutu Travel Limited began in 2021 with a simple dream: to make travel easy, affordable, and stress-free for everyone. What started as a small idea has grown into a global travel agency with branches in Nigeria, China, and beyond, helping people turn their travel dreams into reality.

At Tutu Travel Limited, we know that a great trip isn’t just about where you go but how you get there. That’s why we’ve made it our mission to take the hassle out of travel planning , offering unbeatable ticket prices and a team that’s always ready to go the extra mile for you.

From finding last-minute deals to personalizing every trip to your needs, we’ve built our reputation on exceptional service and a commitment to making every journey smooth and enjoyable . Whether it’s a family vacation, a corporate trip, or a quick getaway, we handle the details so you can focus on the experience.

After all, travel is more than just moving from one place to another, it’s about creating memories, exploring the unknown, and feeling at home wherever you go. At Tutu Travel Limited, we’re here to help you every step of the way.
